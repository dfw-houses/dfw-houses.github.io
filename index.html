About Christine Ballard

Christine Ballard specializes in traditional home sales in Arlington, TX. A local resident of Arlington herself, she has intimate knowledge of the area and a strong desire to make her home, your home. She represents eXp Realty, LLC where she has countless resources at her fingertips to help guide her sellers and buyers through the transaction.

Christine has extensive digital marketing training that allows her to craft innovative and effective advertising campaigns that result in the best possible offers for her home-seller clients. Her business is built on open communication and transparency to provide the smoothest transaction as possible for both her buyers and sellers.
